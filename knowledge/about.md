# About Roman Martins

Roman Martins is a product leader who actually builds AI. Based in Denmark, he combines 10+ years of engineering and technical leadership with 4+ years of hands-on AI product development.

## Career Arc

Roman's career spans three distinct chapters:

**Chapter 1 — LEGO (6 years):** Started as a Processing Technician and grew into a Lead Professional and Global Team Lead in LEGO's Concept Factory. This is where he learned manufacturing at world-class scale, cross-functional coordination, and what it means to ship physical products with zero tolerance for defects.

**Chapter 2 — Engineering & Consulting (5 years):** Moved into senior mechanical engineering and global team leadership at DIS/CREADIS, leading cross-functional teams on complex industrial projects. Then founded a 3D printing startup and an AI product consulting practice.

**Chapter 3 — AI Products (2023–present):** Built an AI-powered personalised children's book platform that shipped 1,000+ books in Germany. Currently a Product Development Manager at JENSEN Group leading a 10-person team while building AI tools and systems independently.

## What Drives Him

Roman sits at the intersection of deep technical understanding and product strategy. While most PMs talk about AI strategy, he ships Claude API tools, designs prompt architectures, and runs AI workflows in production. That practitioner lens — understanding both the product "why" and the technical "how" — is what defines his approach.
