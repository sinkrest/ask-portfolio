# Publishing Suite — Tools for Creator Platforms

A set of tools Roman built to explore the independent publishing and creator-economy space as a product domain. Rather than reading about the space, he built for it — each tool solves a real problem a publisher, newsletter creator, or platform PM actually has. The suite is one of his signature pieces: it shows how he approaches a new domain by shipping, not theorising.

## Why It Exists

Roman was curious about how independent creator platforms really work — where the pain points are, what data signals matter, and where AI can compress existing workflows. So he built. Each tool is small, focused, and live in production. Taken together they form an operating picture of the domain: what creators ship, what their audiences do, what competitors release, and where the strategic gaps sit.

This is the same pattern he applies in manufacturing with the Forkable Factory: pick a domain, build tools inside it, let the work teach you.

## The Tools

### Product Signal
**Live:** ghost-pm-signal.vercel.app | **GitHub:** github.com/sinkrest/ghost-pm-signal
PM intelligence dashboard aggregating 6 live data sources (forum votes, GitHub feature requests, Fediverse/ActivityPub signals, Hacker News sentiment, competitor changelog, platform releases). Claude synthesises everything into a four-section PM decision brief — top opportunities, competitive gaps, strategic signals, and what to deprioritise. Full case study available.

### Content Advisor
**Live:** ghost-creator-advisor.vercel.app
Enter publication metrics (subscribers, open rate, revenue) and get a business diagnosis with specific recommendations. Built for creators who want data-driven guidance instead of generic advice.

### Content Repurpose
**Live:** ghost-repurpose.vercel.app
Newsletter content repurposing — paste a newsletter and get five formats: Twitter thread, LinkedIn post, podcast script, re-engagement email, and summary. The key feature is voice preservation — the tool analyses the creator's writing style and maintains it across formats.

### Competitive Intel
**Live:** ghost-intel-five.vercel.app
Platform intelligence dashboard tracking releases, changelog entries, and AI-generated digest. What the platform ships, summarised for decision-makers.

### Strategy Radar
**Live:** ghost-strategy-radar.vercel.app
Interactive hexagonal radar mapping six strategic pillars (Social Web, Analytics, Monetization, Automation, Discovery, Community) on four dimensions each — maturity, momentum, community demand, competitive parity. Dual-polygon overlay shows demand vs. current state gaps.

### Creator Journey Map
**Live:** ghost-creator-journey.vercel.app
Maps the full creator lifecycle — discovery, onboarding, habit, growth, monetisation — into a visual journey with stage-by-stage signals and intervention points.

### Discovery Tree Experiment
**Live:** ghost-discovery-tree.vercel.app | **GitHub:** github.com/sinkrest/ghost-discovery-tree
Opportunity Solution Tree (Teresa Torres framework) applied to an independent publishing platform. Two outcomes, five opportunities, six solutions, six experiments — all grounded in real signals. Score opportunities, track assumptions, manage a signal inbox. Claude generates strategic briefs.

## The Narrative

Three layers of intelligence, stitched together:

- **Competitive Intel** tracks what the platform ships (the past)
- **Product Signal** tracks what users want and the market signals (the present)
- **Discovery Tree Experiment** structures signals into prioritised decisions (the future)

Content Advisor, Content Repurpose, Creator Journey Map, and Strategy Radar round out the creator-side and strategy-side of the suite.
