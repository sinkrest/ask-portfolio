# Portfolio — Live Deployed Tools

Roman has built and deployed 15+ AI-powered tools to production. All are live, publicly accessible, and most are open source on GitHub. This is not a slide deck portfolio — these are working products.

## Manufacturing × AI — The Forkable Factory

Roman's flag-planting research project on bringing software-style development loops to physical products. Published publicly at romanmartins.com as a living body of work.

### State of Manufacturing APIs (FF API Probe)
**Live:** romanmartins.com/manufacturing-apis | **RSS:** /api-probe-rss.xml
Weekly leaderboard scoring 7 digital manufacturing vendors (Xometry, Protolabs, Hubs, PCBWay, JLCPCB, Shapeways, Fictiv) on 8 agent-readiness dimensions — 100 points total. Answers the question "can an AI agent actually use this manufacturing API yet?" Feeds a public changelog and weekly insight posts. This is the kind of asset that only someone fluent in both APIs and factories can produce.

### Forkable Factory Strategy & Reference Implementation
**Research:** Public research log, build logs, and strategy manual on romanmartins.com
Roman runs a public Explore → Build → Share loop on a reference hardware product (USB heated mug) to prove that agentic environments can drive physical product development. Build logs, market signals, asymmetric-needs research, and compliance-navigator work all live in public.

## PM Tools (Roman's Lab)

### Stakeholder Lens
**Live:** stakeholder-lens.vercel.app | **GitHub:** github.com/sinkrest/stakeholder-lens
Translates a product roadmap into stakeholder-specific language. Input a roadmap item and get tailored messaging for engineering, sales, executives, and support — each in their language, focused on what they care about. Directly applicable inside OEMs where the same roadmap item has to be re-explained to Production, Quality, Automation, and Service.

### AI Implementation Scoping Tool
**Live:** ai-scoping-tool.vercel.app | **GitHub:** github.com/sinkrest/ai-scoping-tool
Two-phase discovery tool: Claude generates targeted questions about a business problem, then produces a full implementation scoping document with AI approach, phased rollout plan, risks, and success metrics. Built to codify the "we want to use AI" → "here's what to actually build" handoff — the same gap that exists in most industrial companies today. Full case study available.

### AI Opportunity Scanner
**Live:** ai-opportunity-scanner.vercel.app | **GitHub:** github.com/sinkrest/ai-opportunity-scanner
"Where should your company use AI?" — Input a business description and get a ranked opportunity map with impact/effort analysis. Helps organisations (including manufacturers) identify their highest-value AI use cases without boiling the ocean.

### AI Product Discovery Simulator
**Live:** ai-discovery-simulator.vercel.app | **GitHub:** github.com/sinkrest/ai-discovery-simulator
Interactive product discovery framework tool. Walk through a structured discovery process with AI-guided steps.

## Publishing Suite — Tools for Creator Platforms

A set of tools Roman built to explore the independent publishing and creator-economy space as a product domain. Built to learn, not to pitch — the same pattern he applies when scoping an AI opportunity inside an industrial company. Lives under "Publishing Suite" in Roman's Lab.

### Product Signal
**Live:** ghost-pm-signal.vercel.app | **GitHub:** github.com/sinkrest/ghost-pm-signal
PM intelligence dashboard aggregating 6 live data sources: forum votes, GitHub feature requests, ActivityPub/Fediverse signals, Hacker News sentiment, competitor changelog, platform releases. Claude synthesises everything into a four-section PM decision brief. Full case study available.

### Content Advisor
**Live:** ghost-creator-advisor.vercel.app
Enter publication metrics (subscribers, open rate, revenue) and get a business diagnosis with specific recommendations.

### Content Repurpose
**Live:** ghost-repurpose.vercel.app
Newsletter content repurposing — paste a newsletter and get 5 formats: Twitter thread, LinkedIn post, podcast script, re-engagement email, summary. Voice preservation is the key feature.

### Competitive Intel
**Live:** ghost-intel-five.vercel.app
Platform intelligence dashboard tracking releases, changelog entries, and AI-generated digest — what the platform ships, summarised.

### Strategy Radar
**Live:** ghost-strategy-radar.vercel.app
Interactive hexagonal radar mapping 6 strategic pillars on 4 dimensions each. Dual-polygon overlay shows demand vs. current state gaps.

### Creator Journey Map
**Live:** ghost-creator-journey.vercel.app
Maps the full creator lifecycle — discovery, onboarding, habit, growth, monetisation — with stage-by-stage signals and intervention points.

### Discovery Tree Experiment
**Live:** ghost-discovery-tree.vercel.app | **GitHub:** github.com/sinkrest/ghost-discovery-tree
Opportunity Solution Tree (Teresa Torres framework) for an independent publishing platform. 2 outcomes, 5 opportunities, 6 solutions, 6 experiments — all based on real signals.
