# Portfolio — Live Deployed Tools

Roman has built and deployed 13+ AI-powered tools to production. All are live, publicly accessible, and open source on GitHub. This is not a slide deck portfolio — these are working products.

## PM Tools (Roman's Lab)

### Stakeholder Lens
**Live:** stakeholder-lens.vercel.app | **GitHub:** github.com/sinkrest/stakeholder-lens
Translates a product roadmap into stakeholder-specific language. Input a roadmap item and get tailored messaging for engineering, sales, executives, and support — each in their language, focused on what they care about. Solves the "every PM rewrites the roadmap 4 times a week" problem.

### AI Implementation Scoping Tool
**Live:** ai-scoping-tool.vercel.app | **GitHub:** github.com/sinkrest/ai-scoping-tool
Two-phase discovery tool: Claude generates targeted questions about a business problem, then produces a full implementation scoping document with AI approach, phased rollout plan, risks, and success metrics. Demonstrates prompt architecture and structured output design. Full case study available.

### AI Opportunity Scanner
**Live:** ai-opportunity-scanner.vercel.app | **GitHub:** github.com/sinkrest/ai-opportunity-scanner
"Where should your company use AI?" — Input a business description and get a ranked opportunity map with impact/effort analysis. Helps organisations identify their highest-value AI use cases.

### AI Product Discovery Simulator
**Live:** ai-discovery-simulator.vercel.app | **GitHub:** github.com/sinkrest/ai-discovery-simulator
Interactive product discovery framework tool. Walk through a structured discovery process with AI-guided steps.

## Ghost Creator Suite (7 Tools, Built in 2 Weeks)

These seven tools were built specifically for a PM interview at Ghost — the independent publishing platform. They demonstrate execution speed, systems thinking, and the ability to deeply understand a product domain quickly.

### Ghost Repurpose
**Live:** ghost-repurpose.vercel.app
Newsletter content repurposing — paste a Ghost newsletter and get 5 formats: Twitter thread, LinkedIn post, podcast script, re-engagement email, and summary. Voice preservation is the key feature — it analyses the creator's writing style and maintains it across all formats.

### Ghost Creator Advisor
**Live:** ghost-creator-advisor.vercel.app
Enter publication metrics (subscribers, open rate, revenue) and get a business diagnosis with specific recommendations. Built for creators who want data-driven guidance.

### Ghost Intel
**Live:** ghost-intel-five.vercel.app
Platform intelligence dashboard tracking Ghost releases, changelog entries, and AI-generated digest. What Ghost ships, summarised for decision-makers.

### Ghost PM Signal
**Live:** ghost-pm-signal.vercel.app | **GitHub:** github.com/sinkrest/ghost-pm-signal
PM intelligence dashboard aggregating 6 live data sources: Ghost Forum votes (880+ topics), GitHub feature requests, ActivityPub/Fediverse signals, Hacker News community sentiment, Kit competitor changelog, and Ghost's own releases. Claude synthesises everything into a four-section PM decision brief. Full case study available.

### Ghost Pulse
**Live:** ghost-pulse.vercel.app
Admin API analytics dashboard for Ghost publications. Connect a Ghost publication and visualise engagement data.

### Ghost Strategy Radar
**Live:** ghost-strategy-radar.vercel.app
Interactive hexagonal radar mapping Ghost's 6 strategic pillars (Social Web, Analytics, Monetization, Automation, Discovery, Community). Each scored on maturity, momentum, community demand, and competitive parity. Dual-polygon overlay shows demand vs. current state gaps.

### Ghost Discovery Tree
**Live:** ghost-discovery-tree.vercel.app | **GitHub:** github.com/sinkrest/ghost-discovery-tree
Opportunity Solution Tree (Teresa Torres framework) for Ghost. 2 outcomes, 5 opportunities, 6 solutions, 6 experiments — all based on real Ghost signals. Score opportunities, track assumptions, manage a signal inbox. Claude generates strategic briefs.
