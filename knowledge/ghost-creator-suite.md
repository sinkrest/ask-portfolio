# Ghost Creator Suite — 7 Tools Built in 2 Weeks

Roman built seven production tools for Ghost (the independent publishing platform) in two weeks. This suite demonstrates extreme execution speed, deep product domain understanding, and systems thinking.

## Why It Exists

Roman wanted to deeply understand Ghost's product ecosystem — its creators, their pain points, and the competitive landscape. Instead of reading about it, he built tools. Each one solves a real problem Ghost creators or the Ghost product team faces. The goal: understand a domain by building for it.

## The Seven Tools

### 1. Ghost Repurpose (ghost-repurpose.vercel.app)
**Problem:** Creators write one newsletter and manually reformat for other channels.
**Solution:** Paste a newsletter, get 5 formats: Twitter thread, LinkedIn post, podcast script, re-engagement email, summary. The key insight: voice preservation matters more than format conversion. The tool analyses the creator's writing style and maintains it.

### 2. Ghost Creator Advisor (ghost-creator-advisor.vercel.app)
**Problem:** Independent creators lack data-driven business guidance.
**Solution:** Enter metrics (subscribers, open rate, revenue) → get a diagnosis with specific recommendations.

### 3. Ghost Intel (ghost-intel-five.vercel.app)
**Problem:** Hard to track what Ghost ships and how the platform evolves.
**Solution:** Aggregates Ghost releases, changelog, and blog into a digest. What Ghost ships, summarised.

### 4. Ghost PM Signal (ghost-pm-signal.vercel.app)
**Problem:** PM signal is scattered across forums, GitHub, competitors, and community discussions.
**Solution:** Aggregates 6 live data sources. Claude synthesises into a four-section PM decision brief. Full case study available.

### 5. Ghost Pulse (ghost-pulse.vercel.app)
**Problem:** Publication owners lack quick analytics access.
**Solution:** Connect a Ghost publication via Admin API → visualise engagement data.

### 6. Ghost Strategy Radar (ghost-strategy-radar.vercel.app)
**Problem:** No visual tool to map Ghost's strategic position across multiple dimensions.
**Solution:** Interactive hexagonal radar mapping 6 strategic pillars on 4 dimensions each. Dual-polygon overlay shows demand vs. current state gaps.

### 7. Ghost Discovery Tree (ghost-discovery-tree.vercel.app)
**Problem:** Product signals don't connect to decisions without a structured framework.
**Solution:** Teresa Torres' Opportunity Solution Tree applied to Ghost. 2 outcomes, 5 opportunities, 6 solutions, 6 experiments — all based on real signals.

## The Narrative

Three layers of intelligence:
- **Ghost Intel** tracks what Ghost ships (the past)
- **Ghost PM Signal** tracks what users want and the market signals (the present)
- **Ghost Discovery Tree** structures signals into prioritised decisions (the future)

## Key Takeaways

- 7 production-grade tools deployed in 2 weeks — demonstrates execution speed and discipline
- Each tool solves a real problem, not a theoretical exercise
- Shows ability to deeply understand a product domain quickly (Ghost's ecosystem, community, competitive landscape)
- Roman's approach: "Don't study a domain — build for it." — demonstrates how he thinks about product work
- All tools are live and functional — anyone can visit and use them today
